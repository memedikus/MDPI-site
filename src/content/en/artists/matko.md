Graduate of the Moscow Higher Art and Industry School (formerly Stroganov), monumental painting studio of Prof. A.L. Orlovsky.  
Participant in international exhibitions. Works held in collections of the State Russian Museum, Tretyakov Gallery, and museums in Russia and abroad.  
Chair of the Section of Monumental, Decorative & Applied Arts of the Union of Russian Artists.

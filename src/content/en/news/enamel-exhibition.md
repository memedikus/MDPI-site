---
title: "International Exhibition «Enamel Artists of Russia»"
date: "2025-11-15"
---

The Section of MDAI presents a new international exhibition dedicated to the art of enamel. The show features works by artists from Moscow, St. Petersburg, Yekaterinburg, as well as invited artists from France and Japan.

The exhibition will run at the Manege Central Exhibition Hall from December 1–30, 2025.
